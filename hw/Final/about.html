About Thelen - the artist

Thelen is an electronic music artist who makes edm focused on live performance. Thelen doesn't dj, as most electronic artists do. Thelen plays most of his shows through Ableton Live, and utilizes live instruments like guitar and piano.

About Thelen - the producer

Thelen also produces for many other artists. By listening to the artists, and inserting a little of his own style into the tracks, Thelen both creates songs that captivate the fans of the artists, while also satisfying his crave for unique productions.
